Company Background What does your business do? Sulti Tech Co will sell and service all computers to the South Sudan. It will also offer custom-built computers, networking solutions, and in-store computer stations where customers can buy time to browse the Internet, check e-mail, and download their digital photos from their camera memory cards onto discs. What will set Sulti Tech Co apart from the competition is providing our customers all this in one convenient location, our dedication to excellent customer service, and an expedient turn-around time on repairs.
